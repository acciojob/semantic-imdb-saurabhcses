Summary

"Inception" (2010), directed by Christopher Nolan, is a sci-fi thriller that explores the world of shared dreaming. Dom Cobb, a skilled thief, specializes in stealing secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state. He is offered a chance at redemption if he can achieve the impossible task: inception, planting an idea into someone’s mind.
